Welcome to EthnoGuessr

Discover the World Through Faces

Test your knowledge of human phenotypes and geographical distribution in this engaging guessing game. Look at the faces, study their features, and try to pinpoint their origin on the world map!

How to Play

  1. You'll be shown two images representing a specific phenotype from a particular region.
  2. Study the facial features and characteristics of the individuals shown.
  3. Click on the world map where you think these phenotypes are most commonly found.
  4. Get points based on how close your guess is to the actual location!
  5. The closer your guess, the more points you earn.
